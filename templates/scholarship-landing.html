Frequently Asked Questions

What is RTSE? +

RTSE (Rankers Talent Search Exam) is a national level test that assesses student's skills and aptitude. It provides opportunities to win scholarships up to 50% for various courses we offer.

Who is eligible for the scholarship exam? +

Students from Class 7 to Class 12 are eligible to appear for the RTSE 2026 scholarship exam. Both online and offline modes are available.

How can I register for the exam? +

You can register for free by clicking on the "Register for free" button on our website. Simply create your account, fill in your details, and you're ready to take the test.

What is the exam duration and pattern? +

The RTSE 2026 exam is of 20 minutes duration and consists of 20 multiple-choice questions (MCQ) covering various subjects and aptitude areas.

How will I receive my scholarship? +

After qualifying the exam, the secured scholarship will be displayed directly on your screen after you submit the exam. Also you will receive a sms response from our team, the sms will include your test score and scholarship secured and our CTA number.

What is the syllabus for RTSE exam? +

The RTSE exam covers subjects including Mathematics, Science, English, and General Knowledge. The syllabus is designed as per the NCERT curriculum for classes 7-12 and focuses on conceptual understanding and practical application.

When will the results be announced? +

The results for RTSE-2026 will be announced right after you finish your exam. You will receive an SMS notification with your score and scholarship details. You can also check your results on our student portal.

Is there any fee for the exam? +

No, the RTSE exam is completely FREE for all students. There are no registration fees or examination fees. You can register and appear for the exam at no cost.

What are the benefits of RTSE scholarship? +

The scholarship can be used for tuition fees, online courses, study materials, test series, and more. Top performers also get access to exclusive mentorship programs, career guidance sessions, and national-level competitions.

Can I take the exam from home? +

Yes, RTSE is an online examination that can be taken from the comfort of your home. All you need is a stable internet connection and a laptop or desktop computer with a webcam for proctoring.
